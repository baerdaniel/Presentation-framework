Case Studies

Forward-thinking e-commerce

Zanzan is a London-based luxury eyewear brand which produces high-quality, handmade women’s sunglasses in a range of fashion-led designs for a worldwide retail audience. We successfully transformed the brand's website into a fully functioning online shop suitable for mobile devices. The finished site retains Zanzan’s free-spirited character while creating an enjoyable and user-friendly retail experience.

Design and development (with Charles Broskoski)

Customisable Page Layouts

Mario Testino’s art institute MATE in Lima is a not-for-profit centre which aims to support and celebrate Peruvian artists while offering international and contemporary art through exhibitions and events. Together with MarioTestino+ and Future Corp, I have designed and developed a website for MATE that is fresh and engaging and complements Mario Testino’s playful photographic style.
