No Plans


No Plans is a digital agency specialising in web design, brand identity and programming. Our background in design and our interactive expertise gives us a wider perspective, suited for special web projects, extensive branding challenges and digital strategy.

Based in London and New York, No Plans was founded in 2015 by Daniel Pianetti and Daniel Baer. Clients include:

Case Studies

Efficient e-commerce

Zanzan is a London-based luxury eyewear brand which produces high-quality, handmade women’s sunglasses in a range of fashion-led designs for a worldwide retail audience. No Plans successfully transformed the brand's website into a fully functioning online shop suitable for mobile devices. The finished site retains Zanzan’s free-spirited character while creating an enjoyable and user-friendly retail experience.

zanzan.co.uk

Shopfront as homepage: the visually bold lookbook is directly followed by the shop section.

No Distraction: Product photos are always in maximum size. No zooming and no clicking is needed.

Press: Visuals from press articles are paired with direct links to the featured products.

Customisable structures

Mario Testino’s art institute MATE in Lima is a not-for-profit centre which aims to support and celebrate Peruvian artists while offering international and contemporary art through exhibitions and events. Together with MarioTestino+ and Future Corp, No Plans designed and developed a website for MATE that is fresh and engaging and complements Mario Testino’s playful photographic style.

Design and development

mate.pe

Custom page builder: The museum staff create new site sections and completely flexible layouts with ease in Wordpress.

Automated calendar: New events easily are automatically sorted into current, upcoming and past events and exhibitions, based on the local date.

Social online platform

The collaborative research platform Are.na hosts a growing community that interacts by adding, connecting and organising pieces of visual information including images, links, files and text. As a co-founder, Daniel Pianetti has worked with Are.na’s fellow team members on the application’s concept, user experience, design and development.

are.na

Minimally branded to conserve the concept of openness (user before brand), complex functionality distilled to a simplistic interface.

More than 9000 users created 35’207 channels containing over half a million blocks.
